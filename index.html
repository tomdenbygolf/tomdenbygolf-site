The Framework

The Real Life Method™

Every player is different. That's why I built a five-step framework that starts with understanding you — your game, your life, your goals — before we touch a single drill.

1
Discover
We start by understanding your game, your goals and the constraints of your real life. No assumptions, no generic advice.
2
Diagnose
Video analysis and detailed assessment to identify the real root cause of what's holding your scores back.
3
Design
A personalised improvement plan built around how much time you actually have to practise each week.
4
Develop
Structured sessions with clear, measurable progress checkpoints so you always know you're moving forward.
5
Deliver
You take the new skills onto the course and see real scores come down. This is where it all matters.
