Privacy Policy
Tom Denby Golf · Last updated April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tom Denby Golf ("I", "me", "my") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit tomdenbygolf.co.uk or sign up for any of my free resources or coaching services.

1. Who I Am

Tom Denby Golf is operated by Tom Denby, a PGA Qualified Professional based in the United Kingdom. For any privacy-related queries, you can contact me at: tomdenbygolfpro@gmail.com

2. What Information I Collect

I collect the following personal information when you submit a form on this website:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Any swing video footage you voluntarily upload for a free swing review
  • Any additional information you choose to provide in form fields

I do not collect payment information, and I do not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes.

3. How I Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Deliver the free Strike It Pure video series you requested
  • Send follow-up emails as part of the video series sequence
  • Provide a personal swing analysis if you submitted video footage
  • Occasionally send coaching tips, updates, and relevant offers (you can unsubscribe at any time)

I will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. How Your Data Is Stored

Your data is collected via Google Forms and stored in Google Sheets, both of which are governed by Google's own privacy and security standards. Email sequences are delivered via Google Apps Script. Your data is stored securely and access is limited to me personally. I do not use any third-party email marketing platforms at this time.

5. How Long I Keep Your Data

I retain your personal information for as long as you remain on my mailing list or have an active coaching relationship with me. If you request deletion of your data, I will remove it within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data I hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Withdraw consent to email communications at any time by clicking unsubscribe in any email
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk

To exercise any of these rights, please contact me at tomdenbygolfpro@gmail.com

7. Third-Party Services

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. Swing videos are hosted via Cloudinary. Forms and data storage are handled by Google. Each of these services operates under their own privacy policies, which I encourage you to review independently.

8. Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
